Born and raised in bygone Bay Area orchards, I've traveled far and seen much. After graduating from UC Davis in 2008, I joined a tech start-up that sent me to Beijing to train customer service. I loved it and joined the Peace Corps, completing service in Lesotho in 2014.

Since returning from Africa, I was a teacher for 4 years at various schools, and now I'm a coding trainee. I like video games, board games, hiking, reading, and cats.
